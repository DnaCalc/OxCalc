# W041: Core Formalization Release-Grade Successor Verification

## Purpose

W041 continues after the W040 non-promoting direct-verification tranche.

W040 improved the evidence floor and narrowed several direct blockers, but it did not promote release-grade full verification, full formalization, C5, pack-grade replay, Stage 2 production policy, operated assurance services, retained-history service, fully independent evaluator breadth, broad OxFml display/publication closure, public consumer-surface migration verification, callable metadata projection, callable carrier sufficiency, or general OxFunc kernels inside OxCalc.

W041 is the next successor verification lane. Its job is to convert the W040 closure-audit successor scope into concrete proof, model, implementation, operated-service, OxFml/callable, pack, and release-grade verification work. Where direct closure is not possible, W041 must keep exact blockers and successor consequences machine-readable rather than treating accumulated evidence as release-grade proof.

W041 remains a formalization, spec-evolution, and engine-improvement workset. It is not a test against a fixed initial spec. Current specs, TraceCalc behavior, optimized/core behavior, proof artifacts, TLA models, OxFml seam notes, operated-service artifacts, diversity evidence, and pack decisions remain evidence surfaces that can force spec correction, implementation work, exact blocker carry-forward, or promotion only when direct evidence warrants it.
